VEGETABLE SALAD

Benefits of Vegetable Salad

Salad greens contain Vitamin A, Vitamin C, beta-carotene, calcium, folate, fiber, and phytonutrients. Leafy vegetables are a good choice for a healthful diet because they do not contain cholesterol and are naturally low in calories and sodium.

Steps to make Vegetable Salad

  • Put all your mixed greens and spinach in a large bowl.
  • Add in the cucumbers, carrots, bell pepper and tomatoes.
  • Top with the tasty toppings, which are your pumpkin seeds, basil leaves and chickpeas.
  • In a separate bowl, place all dressing ingredients and whisk until combined.
  • Toss all salad ingredients in a medium bowl.
  • Dress and plate your salad.
This easy vegetable salad is packed with a variety of summer produced with all the texture and colors ever want.
